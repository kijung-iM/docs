:::note 

**Meaning of the increase rate colors**

-   <span class='ntrs'>Red</span>: If the rate is negative, it indicates that it has decreased compared to the previous week.
-   <span class='ov5ud10'>Orange</span>: It indicates that the rate is 0% or more and less than 10%.
-   <span class='etrs'>Red</span>: It indicates that the rate is 10% or more, or new data appeared that did not exist in the previous week.

:::
