## Checking the access key{#check-accesskey}

The **access key** is the unique ID to enable the WhaTap service.  

In the installation guide section, select **_Getting the access key_**. After automatic reception of **access key**, proceed to the next step.

:::caution

-   Use of the service is restricted if the **access key** is invalid. You can renew or extend it through ![Channel inquiry icon](/img/ico-help.svg) **_Channel inquiry_** on the upper right of the screen.
-   The **access key** cannot be duplicated for other projects and separate access keys are required also in the **namespace project**.

:::

:::note

After a project has been created, the **_Agent Installation_** page appears automatically. If the **_Agent Installation_** does not appear, select **_Management_** > **_Agent Installation_** on the left.

:::
